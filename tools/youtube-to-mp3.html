Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between 128k and 320k bitrates? +
Bitrate refers to the amount of data processed per second. 320kbps (kilobits per second) provides much richer audio with less compression loss, while 128kbps is highly compressed to minimize file storage sizes.
Why do some video conversions fail? +
YouTube video feeds are constantly updated, and public streaming crawlers may experience network blocks. In these cases, the downloader safely triggers high-quality fallback loops to keep the download sequence responsive.
Can I download copyright music? +
Our tool helps save clips for non-commercial educational purposes or private off-line listening loops. Users are responsible for complying with YouTube's Terms of Service guidelines.
Can I convert long video files? +
Yes, we support conversions of videos up to 120 minutes long. Longer streams may take additional time to parse and compile in the loading bar.
