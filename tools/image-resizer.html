Frequently Asked Questions

Does resizing reduce image quality? +
Scaling down discards pixels to fit the new size, reducing file size. For JPEG/WebP formats, the quality slider controls compression ratios to minimize artifact noise.
Why is PNG quality slider disabled? +
PNG is a lossless compression format, meaning it does not lose detail when compressed. Therefore, the compression quality slider is not applicable for PNG.
Can I upscale small images? +
Yes. The tool supports scaling factors up to 200%. However, note that upscaling will stretch existing pixels, making the final image appear slightly blurred.
