Frequently Asked Questions

How does client-side OCR work? +
It utilizes Tesseract.js, a compiled WebAssembly port of the famous Google Tesseract OCR engine, running directly inside your browser without backend network requirements.
Can I parse handwritten text images? +
While clear printed text yields the best results (up to 99% accuracy), clearly written high-contrast handwriting can also be resolved successfully.
Are there any image size limits? +
Since the process executes inside your local browser memory, highly detailed files up to 25MB are parsed smoothly depending on your system hardware.
