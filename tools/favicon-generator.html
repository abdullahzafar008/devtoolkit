Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of favicon.ico? +
Favicon.ico is a legacy format specifically designed for web browsers tab icons. Renders cleanly at 32x32 pixels to maintain tab aesthetic visibility.
Why do I need a 512x512 PNG? +
High-resolution 512x512 app icons are used by Android Chrome manifests to represent web apps (PWAs) pinned directly onto mobile home screens.
Does this tool support transparency? +
Yes. Uploading transparent PNGs or selecting custom text structures outputs transparent alpha channels cleanly across files.
