Frequently Asked Questions

Does this tool take leap years into account? +
Yes, absolutely. The internal math checks year bounds for leap cycles, adding 366-day increments and adjusting the day counts in February dynamically.
What formulas are used for planetary age? +
We divide your total elapsed Earth days lived by the solar orbital days of each planet (e.g. 87.97 for Mercury, 686.98 for Mars) to compute exact fractional years.
How accurate is the western/chinese zodiac generator? +
Very accurate. It maps the day/month of birth to astrological solar sectors, and translates Gregorian years into matching Lunar animal cycles based on standard indexes.
