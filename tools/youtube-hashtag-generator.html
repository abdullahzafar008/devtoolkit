Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I use more than 15 hashtags on a video? +
YouTube has a strict policy regarding over-tagging. If a single video includes more than 15 hashtags on its description or title, the platform's algorithm ignores all hashtags on that video, and it might flag your video for metadata spam.
How are these hashtags chosen? +
The tool matches your category preset with trending datasets (like #Gaming, #recipe) and combines your custom seed words to produce niche variations, alongside general viral growth tags like #shorts and #youtube.
Will hashtags improve my video search rank? +
Yes. Hashtags make it easier for YouTube's search and recommendation systems to index what your video is about, boosting visibility in related video feeds and search categories.
Do I need to put '#' symbol in seed keywords input? +
No, there is no need. The system sanitizes spaces, symbols, and commas, automatically prefixing every generated tag with a '#' sign.
