Frequently Asked Questions

Will the spun content pass plagiarism checkers? +
Yes. Because the rewriter swaps out high percentages of common words and nouns with vocabulary alternatives, it significantly changes sentence structures, helping pass automated text matching limits.
How do I protect my brand name from being spun? +
Check the "Protect Capitalized Words" toggle. This tells the parser to check for words starting with a capital letter (such as "Google" or "DevToolkit") and bypass them during the synonym swapping phase.
Can I undo a synonym change back to the original? +
Yes, easily! Click the green highlighted spun word in the output editor. The very top option in the dropdown list will display the original starting word. Click it to revert.
What is the maximum article length? +
The tool caps conversions at 5,000 words per spin block. This ensures fast client-side calculations and prevents browser script hangs.
