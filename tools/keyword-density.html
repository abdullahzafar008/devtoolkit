Frequently Asked Questions

Does Google penalize high keyword density? +
Yes, Google penalizes sites that practice "keyword stuffing," which is the practice of loading a webpage with keywords in an attempt to manipulate search rankings. It creates a poor user experience and can lead to ranking drops.
What are stop words? +
Stop words are the most common words in a language (such as "the", "a", "an", "in", "to", "of", "and"). Excluding them is standard practice when analyzing density because they do not carry search context or semantic weight.
