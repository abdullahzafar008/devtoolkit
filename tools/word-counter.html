What is Word Counter?

A word counter online utility is a must-have editor helper that calculates detailed text structures and character limits in real time. Perfect for publishers, copywriters, and content directors, our tool tracks keyword densities to help optimize articles for search indexing constraints.

By monitoring density metrics, writers can align text frequency signals to target topics without keyword stuffing. Readability speeds like reading and speaking duration estimations help content creators adjust length variables for script recording tasks.

All calculations execute locally inside the browser. No input text files are transmitted or logged on databases, offering absolute confidentiality during drafting and copywriting phases.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this word counter really free? ▼
Yes, it is 100% free with no limits on text lengths or calculation frequencies.
Do you store the text I write? ▼
No. All analysis is executed locally on your client machine using JavaScript. Nothing is transmitted over the web.
How is reading time calculated? ▼
Reading time is calculated using an average reading speed of 200 words per minute (WPM).
What is keyword density? ▼
It is the percentage frequency of a specific word relative to the total word count in a text document.
Does it count characters inside spaces? ▼
Yes, the tool displays counts both including white space and excluding white space side-by-side.
Does it support keyboard navigation? ▼
Yes, the editor works seamlessly with browser accessibility controls and standard copy/paste commands.
Why are some keywords ignored in density? ▼
Common stop words (like 'the', 'is', 'at') are filtered out to highlight relevant keywords that describe your topic.
How do I export my text? ▼
You can copy it directly to your clipboard or download it as a plain text (.txt) file using the controls.
