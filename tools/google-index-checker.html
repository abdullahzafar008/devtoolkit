Frequently Asked Questions

Why can I not check Google indexing directly in this tool? +
Google restricts automated scraping queries and CORS policies. Direct automated indexing check APIs do not exist for public client browsers. Generating direct site: links is the most reliable, compliant workaround.
What is the indexation rate metric? +
It is the percentage of successfully indexed URLs compared to the total list size. Maintaining an indexation rate above 85% is a healthy indicator for quality websites.
How does submitting an XML sitemap help? +
An XML sitemap lists URLs you explicitly want indexed. Submitting it via Google Search Console forces Googlebot to prioritize scanning those pathways, resolving pending indexing items.
Is there a link parsing limit? +
We support up to 500 bulk URLs per audit block. Keeping lists small allows quick tab opening and manual index status logging.
