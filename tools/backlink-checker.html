Frequently Asked Questions

What is a referring domain? +
Referring domains are the unique website domains (e.g. `wikipedia.org`) that contain hyperlinks pointing to your site. A single referring domain can contain multiple individual backlinks.
How are DoFollow and NoFollow links handled? +
DoFollow links pass search ranking trust and are highly valued for SEO. NoFollow links are valuable for referral traffic and creating a natural, well-diversified link profile, but do not directly boost Domain Authority.
Why do some backlinks fail to show up in audits? +
Web crawlers must discover and index a page before it shows up in backlink databases. New links can take weeks to appear in global index logs.
Is there a link limit on the CSV export? +
The tool exports up to 100 parsed referring links in the detailed CSV sheet, which is more than enough to establish key anchors and referrer authority metrics for optimization.
