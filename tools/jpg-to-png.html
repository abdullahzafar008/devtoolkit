Frequently Asked Questions

Why convert JPG to PNG? +
PNG is ideal for screenshots, logos, and graphics containing text, as it supports lossless compression and transparency. JPG is better suited for photographic prints.
Does transcoding WebP require external libraries? +
No. Modern web browsers natively support WebP encoding and decoding via HTML5 Canvas standard arrays pipelines.
Are there quality trade-offs? +
Converting to JPG or WebP with low quality settings will reduce file size but introduce pixel artifacts. Keep quality above 80% for clean web layouts.
